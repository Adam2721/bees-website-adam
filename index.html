Bees and Their Importance in Nature

Bees are amazing creatures that play a very important role in pollination. Without bees, there would be no honey to spread on toast and fewer flowers around. These little guys produce honey and live in organised colonies with a queen, workers, and drones.

Bee Facts

  • Bees pollinate about one third of the food we eat.
  • There's over 20,000 known species of bees worldwide.
  • Honey never spoils and can last thousands of years.
  • A honeybee can fly up to 15 miles per hour.
